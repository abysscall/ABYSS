// 04 — Technology

Anonymous. Decentralised.
AI-Native.

Three layers of innovation, engineered as a single protocol. Privacy cryptography, true decentralisation, and on-chain AI — indivisible.

Layer I — Privacy
⬡

Zero-Knowledge Proofs

Transaction validity proven without revealing sender, receiver, or amount. ZK-SNARKs ensure mathematical certainty of privacy.

◈

Shielded Pool

All assets reside in a shielded pool by default. Unlike ZEC — where most transactions are transparent — ABYSS makes shielded mandatory.

⊗

Private Smart Contracts

Inspired by Aleo's model: smart contract execution occurs in private. Computation proofs published on-chain, not the computation data itself.

◎

Stealth Addresses

Every transaction generates a one-time address. No address clustering or transaction graph analysis is possible by any party.

◇

Auditability Option

Voluntary view keys allow selective disclosure for compliance or personal proof — without compromising the privacy of others.

⬦

Private Mempool

Transactions are not visible in the mempool before confirmation. No front-running, no MEV extraction, no surveillance of pending activity.

Layer II — Decentralisation
⬡

Leaderless Consensus

No elected block producers or validators with special privileges. Consensus emerges from the full peer set — no bottleneck, no target.

◎

Permissionless Nodes

Anyone can run a node. No staking minimums designed to exclude. The network grows stronger with every new participant.

◇

On-Chain Governance

Protocol upgrades require community vote. No foundation or team can unilaterally modify the chain after genesis.

Layer III — Artificial Intelligence
◈

AI-Augmented Consensus

AI is embedded at the consensus layer — detecting anomalies, optimising throughput, and flagging malicious patterns in real time, privately.

◈

Personal AI Agent

Every registered user selects an AI Agent bound to their account. Operates on-chain, actions verifiable via ZK, identity and data fully private.

◈

Private AI Inference

AI computations run inside ZK envelopes. The network verifies that computation occurred correctly — without ever seeing the inputs or outputs.

// 05 — Social Layer

A social network
that can't be censored.

ABYSS extends the same privacy and decentralisation primitives used for transactions into a fourth pillar — a social network with no central server, no shadow-banning, and no data harvesting.

01 ——

One Identity, No Leaks

Your social presence uses the same ABYSS wallet address and stealth-address mechanism as your transactions. No separate account to register, link, or have breached.

02 ——

Nothing to Delete

Posts and the social graph live on content-addressed, replicated storage — not a company server. No single party can unilaterally remove what's been published.

03 ——

Speak Privately or Publicly

The same view-key system that enables selective disclosure for transactions lets you choose: post under a shielded identity, or attribute it to yourself. Your call, not the platform's.

04 ——

Visible Moderation

No opaque shadow-banning or invisible corporate policy team. Moderation rules and their enforcement are transparent and governed on-chain by the community.

// Your Agent, On the Feed Too

The same AI Agent.
One more job.

Your personal AI Agent can act inside the social layer too — curating, reposting, or replying within limits you set. It runs under the exact same cryptographic policy object as your financial permissions, so an Agent authorised only to post can never be tricked into spending funds, and vice versa.
